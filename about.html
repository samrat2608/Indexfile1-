wallpaper, textile, carpets and moquette

Founded in 2026, Instabilelab transcends the traditional concept of a wallpaper manufacturer, positioning itself as a true creative hub devoted to the reinterpretation of surfaces. Its vision blends different languages and styles to create wallpapers, fabrics, rugs, upholstered accessories and curtains – an unprecedented aesthetic balance that redefines the concept of interior design.



At the heart of Instabilelab’s philosophy lies constant research into textures, materials and application techniques, supported by advanced technological know-how that makes it possible to reproduce original designs on a variety of media. A focus on sustainability guides every stage of production, enhancing local talent and artisanal skill to achieve high quality products that can be fully customised in size, colour and graphic development.



A true laboratory of creativity, where designers, architects, and print specialists work together in synergy to create unique products and ever-evolving, distinctive design projects.
